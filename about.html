Built on Curiosity, Proven in Practice

Omnivorous Labs was founded by Rich Wasserman after 13 years at Amazon and leadership roles at Facebook/Meta, startups, and midsize tech companies. The insight: most consultants specialize in one methodology. The hardest problems don't respect boundaries.

The name isn't just clever branding. It's a methodology: consume ideas widely - management science, academic research, operational experience - and synthesize them into practical, measurable outcomes.

The "Omnivorous" Approach

Most consultants are carnivores or herbivores. Excellent at one thing, limited when problems don't fit their menu. We're different. We pull from everywhere: Theory of Constraints for prioritization, OKR frameworks for goal-setting, frontline operational experience for execution, technical architecture for systems thinking.

This breadth isn't dilettantism - it's pattern recognition. A logistics constraint applied to product development. A marketplace insight applied to internal operations. The best solutions come from unexpected places.
