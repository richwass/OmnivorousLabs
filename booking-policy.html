Booking Policy

By booking a session with Omnivorous Labs, you agree to the following terms:

Session Types & Payment

Fit Check Sessions

  • Fit check sessions are complimentary 15-minute calls for scope and fit assessment only
  • These sessions are designed to determine if our services align with your needs
  • No work deliverables or consulting advice provided during fit checks

Paid Working Sessions

  • All paid consulting sessions require prepayment before the scheduled time
  • Contact us to discuss longer engagements

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • 24+ hours notice: Full refund for cancellations made 24 hours or more before the scheduled session
  • Less than 24 hours: No refund for cancellations made less than 24 hours before the session
  • No-show: No refund if you do not attend the scheduled session without prior cancellation
  • Rescheduling: Sessions may be rescheduled once at no charge if requested 24+ hours in advance

Confidentiality & Recording

  • All session content and client information is held in strict confidence
  • Session recordings require mutual consent from all parties
  • Without explicit consent, sessions will not be recorded
  • Any materials shared during sessions remain confidential unless otherwise agreed

Taxes & Compliance

  • Washington State sales tax will be collected on all services as required by law
  • Current WA State tax rate applies based on service location
  • Tax rates subject to change based on state and local requirements

Liability & Advice

  • Consulting services provide strategic guidance and advisory support
  • All recommendations are based on available information and professional experience
  • Client retains full responsibility for implementation decisions and outcomes
  • Omnivorous Labs is not liable for business decisions made based on advisory sessions

Contact & Questions

If you have questions about our booking policy or need to request an exception to these terms, please contact us at hello@omnivorouslabs.com.

We review policy exception requests on a case-by-case basis and will respond within 24 hours.
