Engagements

Specific, practical engagements for technical and organizational challenges. Based on 20+ years building systems, leading teams, and fixing what's broken.

Goal-Setting & Strategy

OKR Design & Implementation

OKRs fail when they're just rebranded task lists. We'll help you design goals that actually drive outcomes - starting with shared vision, not your spreadsheet. We'll create focused objectives (5 or fewer), measurable key results with controllable inputs, and a quarterly review process that works.

Deliverables:

  • Current goal-setting audit
  • OKR design for one quarter (company and team level)
  • Measurement framework
  • Leadership workshops
  • Quarterly review cadence

Prioritization & Constraint Analysis

You've got fifty priorities. Which means you have zero. Using Theory of Constraints, we'll identify the single critical bottleneck limiting your throughput. Then reduce focus to 1-2 real priorities. Build a roadmap that addresses actual constraints, not wishlist items.

Deliverables:

  • Constraint identification workshop
  • Prioritization framework
  • Focused roadmap (actually focused)
  • Stakeholder alignment sessions

Decision Tenets Development

Amazon uses tenets to hardcode team vision and preload decisions about tough conflicts. We'll help you create tenets that actually guide decisions - not platitudes that sound good in presentations. We'll test them against real tradeoffs you're facing.

Deliverables:

  • Current decision-making assessment
  • Tenet development workshops
  • Documented tenet set with examples
  • Application training

Engineering & Technical

Engineering Velocity Diagnostic

Where does your engineering time actually go? We'll implement a work visibility framework to categorize time: broken stuff, KTLO, coupling overhead, feature work, and future speed investments. Then identify the real bottlenecks killing your throughput.

Deliverables:

  • Work histogram analysis (6 time buckets)
  • Constraint identification using Theory of Constraints
  • Throughput improvement roadmap
  • Executive communication dashboard

Tech Debt Visibility Framework

Make tech debt measurable and communicable to non-technical stakeholders. We'll help you frame it as tax on throughput and defect likelihood - not just "engineering wants to refactor." Then build an effective prioritization framework.

Deliverables:

  • Tech debt categorization system
  • Impact assessment framework
  • Executive communication templates
  • Prioritization criteria

Agile/Kanban Transformation

Scrum fills capacity. Kanban optimizes flow. If your Scrum process is creating multitasking and local efficiency at the expense of throughput, it's time to switch. We'll help you design Kanban boards with effective WIP limits.

Deliverables:

  • Current process assessment
  • Kanban board design and WIP limits
  • Team training and coaching
  • Flow metrics dashboard

Technical Due Diligence

Pre-acquisition or investment assessment. We'll evaluate technical architecture, team quality, process maturity, and tech debt levels. Flag risks and identify opportunities. You'll get an honest assessment, not a sales pitch.

Deliverables:

  • Technical architecture assessment
  • Code quality and tech debt analysis
  • Team structure and talent evaluation
  • Process and velocity assessment
  • Risk register with remediation roadmap
  • Executive summary

Individual Development

Career & Management Coaching

For managers: practical coaching on team dynamics, prioritization, delegation, and the IC-to-manager transition. For ICs: career planning, skill development focus, and navigating organizational politics.

Deliverables:

  • Career/management constraint identification
  • Personal development roadmap
  • Decision framework for career choices
  • For managers: team effectiveness coaching
  • Ongoing 1:1 coaching sessions
